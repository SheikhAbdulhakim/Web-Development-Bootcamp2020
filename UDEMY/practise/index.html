Steps To Learning Tjweed

The Aart of Tajweed

Tajweed is a set of rules for the correct pronunciation of the letters with all its qualities and applying the various recitations.

Makhari Al-huruuf

Makharijal al-huruuf means a pronunciation of arabic letters according the tajweed rules.

Izhaar

Izhaar is to pronounce the letters from their makhraj, clear and distincty, without any changes.

Ikhfa

Ikhfa means to slightly"hide" , when you see any of these letter, make nasal sound and lenthen for one second

Idgham

Idgham means to merge something into something else
